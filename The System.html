The System

The 7.5 FK Field Pistol concept is based on the following general criteria :

Double Stack Magazine

Double stack magazine fed automatic pistol.

image_aspect_ratio

Military Specs

General dimensions and weight similar to current standard issue military pistols.

settings_input_component

Proprietary Cartridge

Proprietary cartridge and projectile having a proven effective range in terms of accuracy.

Terminal Ballistics

Terminal ballistics of at least 100 meters (110 yards) against targets that weight up to 150 kg (330 lbs) while maintaining low recoil and controllability.

Single Hand

Single hand use ability.

Rapid Sight Adjustment

Rapid acquisition sight system that does not require adjustment within the range spectrum.
