The Folding Stock

Designed exclusively for the 7.5 FK Field Pistol. 

ergonomic design

The folding stock is designed exclusively for the 7.5 FK Field Pistol, the ergonomic design is proprietary and unique to FK BRNO. It is a quick attach/quick detach design requiring no fitting, screws, or latches. The pistol can be fired when the folding stock is either in the open or closed position. The magazine can be changed without detaching the folding stock. It comes in one universal size.

    Specifications

    The 7.5 FK Field Pistol concept is based on the following general criteria :

    Open length

    355 mm  (14")

    Folded length

    Weight

    Material
